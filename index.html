"Room on the Roof" follows the story of Rusty, a sixteen-year-old Anglo-Indian boy, who rebels against societal norms in Dehradun, India. Rich Dad Poor Dad

Nov 12

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" is a personal finance classic by Robert Kiyosaki, contrasting the financial teachings of his biological father (the poor dad) with those of his friend's father (the rich dad). Kiyosaki shares lessons on wealth accumulation, financial literacy, and the mindset required for financial success. Through anecdotes and practical advice, the book emphasizes the importance of financial education and passive income generation for achieving financial independence.

Atomic Habbits

Nov 11

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear explores the power of tiny changes, or "atomic habits," in transforming our lives. Clear presents a framework for building and breaking habits based on small, incremental improvements. Drawing on scientific research and real-life examples, he offers practical strategies for creating lasting behavior change and achieving personal and professional goals. The book emphasizes the importance of consistency, identity-based habits, and the role of environment in shaping behavior.
